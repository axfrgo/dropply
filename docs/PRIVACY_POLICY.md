# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-21

## Overview

Dropply provides a local-first desktop application and an optional hosted cloud service.

The local desktop app is designed to work without requiring an account. The hosted cloud service may collect account and device information when you choose to sign in.

## Information We Collect

For local desktop use:

- content you store locally on your own device
- device-local metadata needed to run the app

For hosted cloud use:

- account identifiers such as email address
- authentication metadata
- device and session metadata
- support and operational logs

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- provide account sign-in and session management
- operate hosted sync and device continuity
- enforce abuse prevention and rate limits
- maintain service security and reliability
- provide customer support

## Local-First Data Model

Local desktop usage stores data on your device first. Hosted features are optional and may involve transmitting metadata and synchronized content through Dropply-operated infrastructure when cloud sync is enabled.

## Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with service providers that help us deliver hosted infrastructure, authentication, email delivery, analytics, billing, or support.

## Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards. No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure.

## Retention

We retain account and hosted service data only as long as necessary to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

## Your Choices

You may:

- use the local desktop app without creating an account
- choose whether to enable hosted sync
- request account deletion subject to operational and legal requirements

## Contact

For privacy questions, publish a contact such as `privacy@dropply.com` before launch.
